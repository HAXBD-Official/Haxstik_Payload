REM    - No missing characters at beginning or end of lines

REM    - Compare typed output with payload source manually

REM    miss keystrokes when injection is too fast.
REM
REM  IF END OF LINE IS CUT OFF:
REM    HAXSTIK STRING buffer may be length-limited. Check firmware
REM    STRING parser for max line length cap.
